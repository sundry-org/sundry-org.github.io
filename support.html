Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sundry?

Sundry is a marketplace where users can list and discover unique items — locally or globally. We help people connect to trade safely and conveniently.

Who handles shipping or delivery?

All trades on Sundry are arranged directly between users. The app does not handle shipping or delivery — it's up to both parties to coordinate.

How can I report a user or item?

If you see something suspicious or inappropriate, you can report it directly in the app, or contact us at support@sundry.gr.

Can I cancel a trade?

Since Sundry facilitates direct user-to-user trades, cancellations depend on mutual agreement. We recommend messaging the other party to discuss.

I found a bug. What should I do?

We're sorry! Please email us with as much detail as possible at support@sundry.gr.
