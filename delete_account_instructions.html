Instructions for Deleting Your Account

If you wish to delete your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Menu.
  3. Select Settings.
  4. Scroll down to the Account section.
  5. Tap on Delete Account.

You will be prompted to log in again for confirmation.

After successfully logging in, your account will be deleted. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to back up any important data before proceeding.
